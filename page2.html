Our Purpose

Here at Carmen's K9 Sweaters we are passionate about dogs and confort in the winter. Many dogs do not have a coat that is appropriate for all elements and we want to make sure that all dogs have options to stay cozy outside. It is also important tous that digs are able to move freely and enjoy our product as much as their family loves the look. Our team will not rest until all dogs are cute and comfy!

A little about us

Founder: Carmen Miller

Carmen has been working in the pet industry since 2009 to perfect the fit and feel of all dog sweaters we produce.

Our Team

Carmen's K9 Sweaters has a large team on stand by to make sure you get the right sweater for your dog as well as creating new and improved designs. They are here to answer any questions or concerns you may have.

Our Testers

It is important to us that your dog loves our product as much as you so we have a team of dogs to test our products and give their approval before we release a product. They are here to ensure confort and mobility in all sweaters!

Benifits

Recycled Material

Our sweaters are made out of recyled material. Here at Carmen's K9 Sweaters we like to keep the earth clean for our dogs ro roam.

Allergen Free

Our sweaters are made so that your dogs skin is protected as well as preventing dog hair from entering the air. Our sweaters help with owners who have dog allergies.

Calming

Dogs get anxious in storms or from seperation so we have made it so our sweaters hug them in a way that calms the dogs and comforts them when they are scared.
